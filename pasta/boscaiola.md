# Pasta boscaiola recipe


## Ingredients

- rigatoni
- pancetta
- peas
- mushrooms
- onions


## Instructions

1. Soften the mushrooms. In a medium saucepan, stir together the broth and dried porcini. Bring the stock to a simmer over high heat. Then reduce to low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes or until the mushrooms are tender.
2. Heat the pasta water. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of generously-salted water to a boil for the pasta.
